1. Live Business Prediction

Summary:

This feature analyzes previous gem inventory records stored in the local SQLite database.
When the user enters details for a new gem, the system predicts:
Expected selling price
Expected profit
Expected expenses
Expected selling time
Predictions are calculated using historical averages and trends from similar gem records.
The prediction updates automatically as the user changes the gem details.
A confidence level (Low, Medium, High) is shown based on the amount of historical data available.

Benefit:

Helps users estimate the potential outcome of a purchase before adding it to the inventory, supporting better business decisions.
2. Business Analytics Dashboard

Summary:

This feature converts historical inventory data into meaningful business insights.
It displays:
Total profit and expenses
Monthly revenue and profit trends
Top-performing gem types
Average selling time
Charts and business statistics
The system also generates simple insights, such as the most profitable gem type or the best-performing month.

Benefit:

Helps users understand their business performance, identify trends, and make informed decisions based on historical data.

Overall: Both features work completely offline using SQLite and provide intelligent, data-driven insights without requiring cloud services or external AI models.
